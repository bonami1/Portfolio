Specifications

Duration : from 01/05/23 to ...

Purpose : Develop a transportation management for every user from the choice of the plane tickets to the transportation in car from the aerport to the client's destination.

Programming languages : Java (JavaFX, JDBC)

Softwares : IntelliJ

Presentation : EZ LINE is a transportation agency which, through a desktop application, enables customers to arrive at their chosen destination in complete peace of mind and safety. Agency employees can create and manage customer journeys, and assign a driver. Depending on the customer's request, the employee can create several routes, such as an outward and return trip. The customer can choose whether or not to carpool, in order to reduce the cost of the trip. The driver can then notify the agency of the customer's arrival at the destination.

Fonctionnalities : pick the best plane ticket according to the clients' need, choose a appropriate car location / taxi
